## Key Notes / Traps

### Linus at Epsilon Eridani Apr/2165
The CSV has `April 2165,Linus,Epsilon Eridani,Apr/2165,` — likely a mistake. Linus departed EE in September 2150 (part29) and arrived at Epsilon Indi in April 2165 (part47, 14.5 year journey). He was NOT at EE in April 2165. The correct entry is `April 2165,Linus,Epsilon Indi,Apr/2165,`. However, removing the erroneous EE entry would cause the map to draw Linus traveling from Epsilon Indi to... nowhere (he has no other future system). Consider leaving it as a "departure point" approximation, but it is technically wrong.

### Goku at Alpha Centauri Nov/2163
Calvin (AC-B) and Goku (AC-A) are both physically in the Alpha Centauri binary system. Only Calvin was in the original CSV. Goku added.

### Luke departs for Kappa Ceti, Bender for Gamma Leporis A (Oct 2165)
Neither Kappa Ceti nor Gamma Leporis A is in `SYSTEM_COORDS`. Luke and Bender are not tracked beyond their Delta Eridani entries. On the map they will appear stationary at Delta Eridani after their last entry — this is imprecise but the systems they traveled to are not in the app's coordinate set. Neither character appears in Books 2 or 3.

### Khan assault on 82 Eridani — naming inconsistencies in Ch60
The chapter names "Kyle" and "Ned" in early strategy dialogue, then names "Jackson" and "Hector" as death victims. The total is 8 Bobs (count confirmed). Kyle/Ned are the same characters as Hector/Jackson (authorial error in the chapter). Added Fred, Jackson, Hector as the 3 missing dead Bobs. Kyle and Ned are NOT separate Bobs.

### Aug 2165 — Luke on shakedown
part44 says "Luke was on a shakedown cruise, and lightspeed lag would make him ineffective" during the gorilloid attack. Luke may have been elsewhere in the DE system. Did not add Aug 2165 PIT for Luke; his last DE entry is Jul 2165.

### Garfield stays at Epsilon Eridani
Garfield was activated in Dec 2145 and remained at EE with Bill throughout Book 1. His continuous presence is implied by the map interpolation between his Dec 2145 entry and next Book 2 entries. No gap entries needed.
